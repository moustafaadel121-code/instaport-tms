## RECORDING TIPS
1. **Speak slowly and clearly** — pause 1 second between sentences
2. **Move the mouse slowly** — viewers need time to follow
3. **Use zoom-in** on important buttons before clicking
4. **Add subtitles** after recording (use Kapwing or CapCut — free)
5. **Add background music** at 10–15% volume (use Pixabay free music)
6. **Keep it under 5 minutes** — attention drops after that
